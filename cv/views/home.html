I am an educated software developer and continuous integration specialist, working in the enterprise Microsoft (.Net/MSSql), Linux (Java/Oracle) and agnostic (Mongo/Elastic/Angular/Node) arenas.

I am happy to work with teams who aspire to utilise better technologies. I will also work with teams who do not embrace these ideas, if the money or location is exceptional or if the assignment is brief and focused.
